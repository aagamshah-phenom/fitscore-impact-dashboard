The key insight: The language gap — which previously prevented scoring for a significant share of hires — is resolved by PMI's Gen3 migration. The only remaining coverage gap is incomplete job configuration. Closing that plus engaging the A/B Lead pipeline are the two actions that get PMI to 80%.
Two Actions to Reach 80%
In order of speed and impact
Action 1 — Engage the A/B Lead Pipeline Immediate · No tech changes
31,047 A or B rated candidates are sitting at Lead — already identified by Fit Score as strong matches but not yet engaged by a recruiter. Prioritising outreach to this group can begin this week. No model changes, no configuration work, no waiting. This is the fastest lever to improve hiring outcomes and move the A/B hire rate this quarter.
Action 2 — Complete Job Configurations 1,088 hires currently unscored
12% of hires had no Fit Score because the job posting was incomplete — required skills, experience range, or location were not filled in. Fit Score needs a minimum level of job information to generate a rating. Identifying jobs with incomplete configuration and coaching recruiters on setup best practices closes this gap without any system changes on either side.
✓ Language Coverage — Already Resolved by Gen3 No action needed from PMI
Before Gen3: A significant share of candidates could not be scored because their profile or job was in a language the previous model did not support. In the Jan–Jun 2026 data window, 2,052 hires fell into this category — candidates who were hired but had no Fit Score to guide the decision.

With Gen3 (active now): PMI completed its Gen3 migration last month. Gen3 includes full cross-lingual scoring across 17 languages — English, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Dutch, Chinese, Italian, Polish, Japanese, Turkish, Romanian, Czech, Serbian, Korean, Hungarian, and Finnish. Candidates in these languages are now scored automatically. No configuration change is needed from PMI's side.

Impact: The 2,052 previously unscored hires from language gaps will no longer occur under Gen3. This alone expands the scoreable candidate pool and improves the overall A/B hire rate going forward.
Combined impact: Gen3 has already eliminated the language scoring gap — 2,052 previously unscored hires per period will now receive a Fit Score automatically. Engaging the 31,047 A/B Lead candidates improves outcomes this week. Closing the job configuration gap adds the final coverage boost. PMI is closer to 80% than the Jan–Jun numbers suggest — the path forward is operational, not technical, and one gap is already closed.
