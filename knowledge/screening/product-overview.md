# Screening — Product Overview

## What Exists Today

Phenom Screening is a **multi-tier automated screening suite** that replaces manual recruiter phone screens and form-based questionnaires. There are three product tiers that ladder up in automation and AI capability:

* **Basic Screening** (included with CRM): KO/MCQ/open text questions; auto-invites via email/SMS; branded landing and thank-you pages; auto-disposition for KO answers; recruiter evaluation in CRM. Fast to configure, immediate ROI.
* **X+ Screening** (advanced tier): Adds video/audio questions, file uploads, scored items with rubrics; AI-powered questionnaire generation from JD and skills; transcription & X+ insights; workflow automations (auto-assign questionnaires, reminders, forwards, status updates).
* **Voice Agent Screening** (generally available, formerly beta): Two-way conversational AI agent via phone call, callback, or browser. The agent conducts a natural conversation, handles interruptions, retains context, probes on answers, and enforces guardrails. Outputs structured summaries, annotated transcripts, recommended ratings, and triggers next-step automation.

## Screening Types

### Standard Screening (Basic & X+)

Candidates receive an invitation link via email/SMS and complete a self-paced questionnaire on a branded landing page. For X+ customers, video/audio responses are recorded and reviewed. The recruiter sees all responses in the CRM Screening tab, filtered by qualification status and AI rating. High-volume use cases rely on KO + rating for triage; knowledge-worker roles typically involve deeper review of video clips and transcripts.

### Conversational AI Screening (Voice Agent)

The Voice Agent initiates an outbound call (or accepts inbound/callback) and conducts a structured but conversational interview. Key differentiators:

* Knowledge-base grounding: agent answers FAQs about the role
* Context memory: tracks prior answers to probe intelligently
* Interruption handling and noise/echo suppression
* Graceful disconnects with callback options
* Candidate is always informed they are speaking with an AI agent
* Outputs: structured summary, annotated transcript, recommended rating, automated next step (schedule, forward, KO-route)

**Voice Agent adoption data:** 85%+ completion rate; ~90% of screenings completed within 1 day; ~20% KO rate; 4.5/5 candidate satisfaction; <1% opt-out.

## User Roles

| Role | Key Actions |
| --- | --- |
| **Recruiter** | Builds questionnaires; enables screening on jobs; configures auto-assignment rules and invitations; reviews evaluations in CRM Screening tab; triggers manual invites; manages forwarding to HM; acts on KO and ratings; sets automations for next steps. |
| **Hiring Manager** | Optionally receives forwarded screenings (configurable per customer); reviews candidate video/transcript clips; submits evaluation scores. HM involvement level is customer-configured: some HMs see everything, others only flagged candidates. |
| **HR Admin** | Configures global settings: consent language, media deletion policies, evaluation permissions, landing page branding, CMS pages, email templates. Manages user roles and access. |
| **Candidate** | Receives email/SMS invite; completes screening on branded landing page (web) or via phone (Voice Agent); can request callback or reschedule; submits opt-out if preferred. Perceived as positive: faster process, fewer rounds, fairer evaluation. |

## Known Limitations / Adoption Reality

* **Adoption:** ~25% of Phenom customers use Screening (Basic + X+). Balanced US/EU adoption. The go-to-market motion is shifting from upsell to _land product_ — position Basic Screening as a fast start with immediate ROI.
* **Voice Agent:** Available but still expanding coverage to more role types. Works best for high-volume, structured roles. Knowledge-worker roles require more configuration of question rubrics.
* **HM involvement:** Configurable but varies widely by customer. Some customers don't expose screening to HMs at all; others route everything through HM approval.
* **Change management:** The main adoption barrier is recruiter/HM behavior change — trusting AI-recommended ratings and automating next steps. Customers need enablement support to move beyond manual review of every screening.
