# Screening — Strategy

## Pod Vision

Replace manual recruiter phone screens with automated, intelligent screening across all role types — from high-volume frontline to knowledge-worker roles. Screening is the **entry point** to the Hiring Intelligence suite: fast to configure, immediate ROI, and the trigger that sets the rest of the hiring process in motion.

North star: _every active job has a screening touchpoint, and the output drives what happens next automatically._

## Strategic Priorities (Current Half)

1. **Voice Agent expansion:** Extend Voice Agent Screening to more role types and customer segments. Move from beta/limited to broadly available, with proven completion rates (85%+) and candidate satisfaction (4.5/5). The goal is replacing manual phone screens at scale.
2. **Land motion (not just upsell):** Position Basic Screening as a fast-start entry product with immediate ROI — configurable in hours, not weeks. Drive new logo adoption and reduce dependency on Scheduling as the sole entry point.
3. **Connect screening insights to outcomes:** Link screening ratings and KO results to downstream hiring outcomes (offer acceptance, 90-day retention, performance). Close the feedback loop so AI recommendations improve over time.

## Roadmap Themes

1. **Short-term (now – 2 quarters):** Voice Agent coverage expansion; improve rubric/rating quality; reduce setup friction for new customers; better HM access options.
2. **Mid-term (2–4 quarters):** Screening insights linked to outcomes (performance/retention data); advanced analytics surfacing ROI in-product; deeper Automation Engine integration for complex routing logic.
3. **Long-term (4+ quarters):** Interview Agents for automated specific interview rounds (case, skills, structured); fully agentic end-to-end for certain role types; Fraud Detection Agent integrated post-screening.

## How This Pod Fits the Platform

Screening sits at the **top of the hiring funnel** and is the first automated intelligence touchpoint after application:

* **Inputs from:** ATS/CRM job creation (triggers auto-assign of questionnaires via Automation Engine); candidate applications (triggers auto-invite).
* **Outputs to:** Scheduling (auto-trigger interview scheduling on screening pass); Interview Intelligence (screening summary informs interview prep); CRM (status updates, ratings, evaluations).
* **Platform dependencies:** Automation Engine for conditional routing; CRM for requisition context and recruiter workflows; Communication Engine for email/SMS delivery.
