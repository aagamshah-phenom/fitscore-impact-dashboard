## How the Three Products Relate

| Product | What It Does | Relationship |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Scheduling** | Automates interview coordination — invite, confirm, reschedule, escalate | Independent product; the entry point into the suite. Triggers the interview; optionally auto-enables recording. |
| **Structured Interviews** | Provides the framework for consistent, fair evaluation — guides, scorecards, question library | Foundational layer. Standalone value for consistency + compliance. Required for Interview Intelligence to deliver its full capability. |
| **Interview Intelligence** | AI enhancement layer that records, transcribes, and analyzes interviews | Builds on Structured Interviews. Amplifies the value of guides and scorecards with AI insights. Does not replace human evaluation. |

## Suite North Star

_Every interview at a Phenom customer is scheduled automatically, conducted with a structured guide, and enhanced by AI — resulting in faster decisions, fairer evaluation, and a defensible audit trail._

## Suite Success Metrics (North Star)

Primary: **% of active jobs where at least 2 interview management tasks are fully automated** (scheduling confirmed, scorecard assigned and submitted, evaluation turnaround <48 hrs).

Guardrails:

* **Time-to-hire delta:** Median % reduction for jobs using Interview Management vs. not.
* **Intelligence coverage:** % of interviews with an AI-generated summary surfaced and used.
* **Evaluation completeness:** % of interviews with a submitted scorecard within 48 hours.

## Maturity Curve

Customers typically progress through adoption stages:

1. **Scheduling only** — automate coordination, reduce no-shows
2. **+ Structured Interviews** — add guides and scorecards; consistent evaluation criteria
3. **+ Interview Intelligence** — enable recording; surface AI summaries and bias detection
4. **Agentic** — Coach Agent, Evaluation Agent, fully automated interview rounds for specific role types
