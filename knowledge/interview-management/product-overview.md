## The Three Products

| Product | Core Function | Adoption | Standalone? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Scheduling** | Automates interview coordination: invite, confirm, reschedule, escalate, follow-up | ~300+ customers. Most adopted HI product. Frequently the suite entry point. | Yes — fully standalone. No dependency on other products. |
| **Structured Interviews** | Interview guides, scorecards, question/attribute library, evaluation forms | Available to all customers. Adoption grows as Interview Intelligence is adopted (prerequisite). | Yes — valuable without recording for consistency and EEOC compliance. |
| **Interview Intelligence** | AI recording, transcription, summaries, bias detection, question coverage, playback | ~15% of online interviews recorded on average. 2–3 all-in logos at 95%. | No — requires Structured Interviews (guides/scorecards) for full value. |

## How They Work Together

The typical integrated flow:

1. Recruiter creates a **Scheduling** request → candidate self-selects a time slot.
2. Confirmed meeting has a **Structured Interview** guide and scorecard assigned.
3. If **Interview Intelligence** is enabled: recording bot auto-joins; consent is captured; AI begins analysis.
4. Post-interview: AI summary and coverage check available; interviewer submits scorecard (with AI assistance); HM reviews forwarded recording + evaluation.
5. Evaluation submitted → CRM/HRM updated → next stage triggered via Automation Engine.

## Key Differentiator

Point solutions exist for each of these capabilities independently. Phenom's advantage is that all three are **natively integrated in one platform** alongside Screening, the CRM/HRM, and the Automation Engine. The connected signals (screening insights informing interview prep, scheduling confirming recording consent, evaluation data feeding hiring analytics) are only possible in an integrated suite.
