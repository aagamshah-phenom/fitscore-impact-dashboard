# Interview Intelligence — Strategy

## Pod Vision

Make every interview smarter without changing how interviewers work. Interview Intelligence is the **AI enhancement layer** on top of Structured Interviews — it records, transcribes, and analyzes interviews, then surfaces insights that help evaluators move faster, decide better, and comply without effort. The interviewer stays in control; the AI handles the cognitive overhead.

North star: _every structured interview produces an AI-assisted evaluation summary, a bias-aware compliance check, and a searchable record — automatically, without incremental effort from the interviewer._

## Strategic Priorities (Current Half)

1. **Close the adoption gap:** Average recording rate is ~15% of online interviews; 2–3 logos are "all-in" at 95%. The bottleneck is change management, not product capability. Priority is structured enablement playbooks, in-product adoption analytics for admins, and friction reduction in the recording consent flow.
2. **Surface ROI clearly in-product:** The strongest resonating value story is consistency & risk reduction, followed by time savings. But customers want to _see_ the time saved — not hear about it in a deck. Build an in-product ROI dashboard: time saved per interview, evaluation turnaround improvement, bias detections surfaced.
3. **Strengthen the intelligence layer:** Summaries, question coverage, and bias detection are live. Next priorities: better summary configurability (role-specific templates), highlight reels for fast review, and "Ask Anything" across single or multiple interviews. These deepen differentiation from point solutions like BrightHire and MetaView.

## Roadmap Themes

1. **Short-term:** In-product adoption analytics for admins; ROI dashboard (time saved, evaluation turnaround); improved summary templates; highlight reel generation.
2. **Mid-term:** "Ask Anything" conversational interface across interview history; interviewer coaching feedback loops from recorded content; configurable bias criteria per customer; cross-interview search and pattern detection.
3. **Agentic path (12–18 months):** **Interviewer Coach Agent** — real-time guidance during interviews, post-interview coaching, flagging of missed questions or bias risks. **Evaluation Agent** — drafts evaluations based on recording + guide; evaluator reviews and approves. **Interview Agent** for specific automated round types (likely aligned with Screening team evolution).
4. **Long-term:** Extend beyond hiring — Growth Intelligence: onboarding conversations, performance check-ins, manager coaching. Interview Intelligence becomes the organizational memory for structured conversations.

## Competitive Context

Primary competitors in the standalone recording/intelligence space: **BrightHire** and **MetaView**. Phenom's advantage is **integration-first** — II is embedded in the same platform as Scheduling, Structured Interviews, Screening, and the CRM/HRM. Point solutions can record; only Phenom connects recording insights to the full hiring workflow.

Win strategy: deepen intelligence capabilities (coaching, configurable insights, agentic path) while the integration advantage grows harder to replicate.

## How This Pod Fits the Platform

* **Builds on Structured Interviews:** Guides and scorecards are the baseline; II enhances them with AI coverage checks, bias detection, and faster evaluation submission.
* **Triggered by Scheduling:** Confirmed meetings auto-enable recording consent when configured. The scheduling → II handoff is automatic.
* **Feeds CRM/HRM:** Recordings, summaries, and evaluation results stored at requisition/candidate level. Searchable and auditable.
* **Governance guardrail:** AI augments evaluators; no automated hire/reject decisions. Final say is always human.
