# Interview Intelligence — Product Overview

## What Exists Today

Phenom Interview Intelligence is the AI enhancement layer that amplifies the value of Structured Interviews. It records, transcribes, and analyzes interviews — surfacing insights that help evaluators move faster, decide better, and comply without effort. **Interview Intelligence does not evaluate candidates**; it augments human judgment with AI assistance. The interviewer and evaluator remain in full control.

## Core Capabilities

### Recording & Transcription

* Auto-joins video interviews as a meeting bot participant (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams)
* Phone interview recording supported
* Audio-only mode supported (no video analysis)
* Full transcript generated and searchable post-interview
* Speaker timelines with diarization (who said what, when)

### Companion App (Interviewer Experience)

A real-time dashboard for interviewers during the interview:

* Access assigned interview guide and customize questions before/during
* View candidate profile and application details
* Automated highlights and note capture during the conversation
* Submit scorecard evaluation at the close of the interview
* Integrated with Zoom and Teams for in-platform access

### Playback & Collaboration

* Full recording playback with speed control
* Speaker timelines and transcript search
* Mark highlights and red flags at specific timestamps
* Add comments and tags; share with colleagues
* Granular access control — share/revoke per interview, per person
* Forward interviews to hiring managers with controlled access

## Privacy, Consent & Compliance

* **Candidate consent:** Candidate is informed multiple times that the interview will be recorded. Can opt out before or during the call. Opt-out rate is <1% in practice.
* **Consent configuration:** Implicit opt-in or implicit opt-out; configurable per country/region to meet local legal requirements.
* **Data residency:** Recordings stored in-region per tenant configuration.
* **PII handling:** PII anonymized before intelligence processing.
* **Retention:** Configurable retention windows by region; data deleted when recording is stopped or retention expires.
* **Access control:** Admin vs. User roles; per-interview sharing with full audit trail; revocable access.

## Adoption Reality

* **2–3 customers** record ~95% of their interviews ("all-in" logos).
* **Most customers** hover around ~20% adoption in knowledge-worker contexts.
* **Average across base:** ~15% of online interviews recorded.
* **Phone recording** capability exists but is under-adopted; the primary barrier is change management, not technology.
* **Primary value story** resonating with buyers: consistency & risk reduction first, time savings second. Customers want to see time savings surfaced in-product (not just in a deck).
