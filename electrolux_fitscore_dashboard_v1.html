How we calculated this
Step 1
Count scored candidates
We take every candidate-job pair where FitScore returned A, B, C, or No Fit. Error states (Incomplete Job, Incomplete Profile, Unsupported Language) are excluded.
657,600 pairs
Step 2
Apply time savings
A/B candidates: 2 min saved each — recruiter can act faster knowing they're a priority. C/No Fit: 4 min saved each — recruiter avoids full CV review for low-fit candidates.
1,861,200 min saved
Step 3
Convert to hours & cost
Total minutes ÷ 60 = hours saved. Hours × €30/hr (Electrolux benchmark rate) = total cost saved.
31,020 hrs → €930,600
Step 4
Express as FTE
Hours saved ÷ 1,800 productive hrs/FTE/year. Represents real headcount capacity unlocked for higher-value recruiting work.

Unscored candidates — opportunity to unlock
Some candidates could not receive a FitScore due to missing data. These are not errors in the AI — they reflect data completeness issues that are fixable. Resolving these would directly increase the number of candidates recruiters can act on.
Incomplete Job
~27,000
candidates unscored
Root cause: The job posting is missing required fields (skills, title, or experience). FitScore cannot match without this.
Fix: Recruiters complete the job description in CRM — no product change needed. This is the single highest-leverage improvement available.
Incomplete Profile
~62,000
candidates unscored
Root cause: Resume did not parse enough information for scoring (missing skills, experience dates, or title).
Fix: Encourage candidates to complete their profile, or enable profile enrichment via sourcing integrations.
Unsupported Language
~59,000
candidates unscored
Root cause: Resume is written in a language not yet supported in the current FitScore configuration.
Fix: This requires a product configuration update — contact your Phenom team to enable additional languages.
⚡ Quick win: completing job descriptions for roles with missing fields could unlock scoring for ~27,000 additional candidates — immediately increasing FitScore coverage with no technical change required.
